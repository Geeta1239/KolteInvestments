KYC (Know Your Customer)

Check KYC Status

Submit any one from ID/Address, plus a recent photo & signature. Ensure details match PAN.

  • PAN Card (mandatory & clear scan)
  • Aadhaar Card (preferred) OR Passport / Driving Licence / Voter ID — as ID + Address proof
  • Recent Passport-size Photo (JPEG/PNG)
  • Signature on blank white paper (scan/photo)
  • Bank Proof — Cancelled cheque / 1st page of passbook / bank statement with name, IFSC & account number
  • Mobile & Email (linked to Aadhaar preferred)

Tip: If address on Aadhaar is old, update it on myAadhaar before KYC.

CAN Account (Common Account Number)

Create CAN

For investments across multiple Mutual Funds with a single investor profile. Ideal for individuals & families.

  • PAN Card of applicant (mandatory)
  • KYC Status should be Registered (not On-hold). If not, complete KYC first.
  • Address Proof — Aadhaar/Passport/Driving Licence/Utility bill (≤ 3 months)
  • Bank Proof — Cancelled cheque / passbook / statement with IFSC & MICR
  • Nominee Details — Name, relation, DOB (if minor)
  • FATCA/CRS Declaration — Will be filled during CAN creation
  • For Joint / Minor / NRI (if applicable):
    – Joint holders: PAN, KYC & signatures of all holders
    – Minor: Birth certificate & guardian KYC
    – NRI: Passport & overseas address proof
Note: Make sure your name, date of birth and address match across PAN, Aadhaar and bank proof to avoid rejections.

You can share photos/scans of documents. Clear, full-frame images work best.
